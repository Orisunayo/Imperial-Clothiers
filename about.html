About IMC

IMC is an eco-friendly cloth manufacturing company established in 2021, we produce our products in our office located in Nigeria.

IMC is an entity with culture, character, charisma and personality and our intention is to be recognise in communities of brand creativity.

IMC is a premium clothing manufacturer that develop exclusive and original lady wears, summer dresses, cocktail dresses, dinner dresses, evening long dresses at your wish and interest to all regions in Nigeria. We offer a unique, interactive experience in which the clients plays an important role in the design process by collaborating with the designers.
